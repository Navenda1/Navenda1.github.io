Some of my Favorite Travel Pictures

Norway
Me and a friend went to the northern region of the Lofoten Islands in Febuary of 2017. To be able to clearly see the northern lights you need to go in between Jan-Mar. The big suprise for me was that the temperature wasn't super cold. Becuase of the air blowing in from the ocean the temps hover just under freezing.


South Korea
For a few years I was stationed on an Air Force base in SK. My favorite trips was getting away from the cities and visiting the national parks.


Philippines
One vacation I did was 3 weeks in PH. It is the most budget friendly place I have traveled to. The southern islands are very much worth seeing and the water is clear.
