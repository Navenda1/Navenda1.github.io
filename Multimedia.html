Youtube!

Alot of my education in this hobby has been from watching Youtube. The two big ones that I found helpfull are “The Art of Photography” and “Tony & Chelsea Northrup”. One focuses on art basics similar to learning painting. Things like lighting and framing. The other channel focuses on the science of how camera equipment gear works and how to effectively use it.

David Yarrow is my favorite professional photographer. I find his most impressive work the close up shots of wildlife. Alot of work and skill is required to get that close to wild animals.
