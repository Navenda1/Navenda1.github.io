Sony RX100
This was the first dedicated camera I owned. This class of camera is usually refered to as a "point and shoot". The best part about this is it will fit in your pocket while still providing a nice quality increase from a phone camera.

Sony A6000
After awhile I wanted an upgrade to a interchangable lens camera. With this style you could get drastic different focal length lens to shoot voomed in or very wide.

Fuji XT-3
This is my most recent upgrade. I mainly picked it over the larger sony cameras because I like having all the buttons on the top of the major settings.
